About me

- I have moved to National Institute of Informatics in Tokyo, Japan since Apr. 2017.

- I was post-doc Researcher in department of Computer Science and Enginieering, Washington University in St. Louis from June 2014 to November 2016 and worked with Dr. Yasutaka Furukawa.

- I have got my Ph. D in 2014 and MS in 2011 in University of tokyo「nder the supervision of Prof. Kiyoharu Aizawa.
